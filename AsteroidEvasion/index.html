Asteroid Evasion

Vote for Asteroid Evasion on Steam Greenlight

Story

You're a space pilot and have been asigned a task. You must deliver information to another planet as fast as possible. The shortest path is right through the asteroid belt. Will you crash or will you deliver? That is up to you, your piloting skill and reaction. Good Luck!


Extra Info

Evade the asteroids, shoot them (providing the current ship can) or crash into them (not advised). On release you will receive at least 1 hour of gameplay.


System Requirements

CPU: Dual Core with 2GHz or better.
OS: Windows XP or newer, Mac OS X 10.6 or newer, Ubuntu 10.10+ or newer, SteamOS
Graphics card: DX9 (shader model 2.0) capabilities; generally everything made since 2004 should work.
RAM: 2GB or more
